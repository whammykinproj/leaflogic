# JobScout AI — Product & Launch Playbook

## One-liner
AI-powered job hunting that works while you sleep.

## Elevator pitch
JobScout AI scans 11+ job boards daily, matches roles to your exact profile using Claude AI, drafts personalized pitches, and delivers everything to your inbox before your morning coffee. $29/mo after a 7-day free trial.

## Target audience
- Tech professionals actively job searching (PM, SWE, VC, Strategy/Ops, CoS)
- 25-40, based in major US metros
- Frustrated with manually checking job boards
- Willing to pay for tools that save them hours/week

## Core value props
1. **Saves 5+ hours/week** — no more scrolling Indeed/LinkedIn/Wellfound
2. **AI-drafted pitches** — copy-paste-ready cover letters and cold emails
3. **Personalized matching** — not keyword spam, actual semantic fit scoring
4. **Full toolkit** — cover letters, interview prep, resume review, application tracker

## Pricing
- 7-day free trial (no card required)
- $29/month (all features, no tiers)
- Referral: +7 days free for both parties

## Key metrics to track
- **Activation**: % of signups who complete onboarding
- **Engagement**: % who open digest emails
- **Retention**: 30-day, 60-day, 90-day
- **Revenue**: MRR, trial→paid conversion rate
- **Viral coefficient**: referrals per user
